Sayid Mohamed Abdulle Hassan

Somali history

Sayyid Mohammed Abdullah Hassan (1856–1920) was a Somali religious leader, poet, and anti-colonial resistance fighter. He is one of the most important figures in Somali history and is often regarded as a national hero for leading a long struggle against British, Italian, and Ethiopian forces in the early 20th century.
